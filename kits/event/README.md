# Event

A high-energy system for conferences, launches, and meetups: crisp light
ground, near-black ink, and a vivid rose accent used boldly across the hero,
CTAs, and schedule tracks. Punchy oversized type and rounded cards give it a
confident, sell-the-ticket feel.

## Composition

Leads with a full-height **EventHero** — name, one-line pitch, date +
location, and the ticket CTA — so the what/when/where lands instantly. The
**Speakers** grid is the social proof, **Schedule** lays out the agenda
session by session, and **Tickets** converts with a highlighted recommended
tier. **Sponsors** adds credibility low on the page, and the **Footer**
repeats the essentials (date, location, code of conduct, contact). The rose
accent recurs throughout to tie the energy together; sections reveal on
scroll.
