# AI Product

A conversion-first design system for AI and developer products, in the classic
dark platform look — the Vercel / Linear / model-lab aesthetic. The canvas is a
neutral near-black, the ink near-white, and the primary action is a confident
white button; a single electric-iris accent is held in reserve for glows,
gradient edges, monospace eyebrows, and the highlighted plan, so it reads as
serious and engineered rather than decorated. Type is Geist for headings and
body with Geist Mono for the technical eyebrows and labels, and faint masked
grids sit behind the hero and closing CTA to suggest a product built on
infrastructure. This kit is dark by design — there is no light counterpart.

This is a marketing page system, not a presentation deck. Every section is a
fluid, full-width band that a downstream caller fills through props: a sticky
navbar with in-page anchor links, a glowing benefit-led hero with a framed
product screenshot, social proof, a capability grid, a customer voice,
usage-based pricing, a closing call to action, and a structured footer. Motion
is deliberately restrained — tasteful scroll-in reveals and gentle staggers —
because the goal is to move a developer toward signing up, not to dazzle them.

## Composition

The chain mirrors how a high-converting developer-platform page builds trust
and pushes toward signup. **Navbar** anchors the page and keeps the primary CTA
in reach at all times; its links jump to the section ids below. **Hero** states
the core promise above the fold over a faint grid and an iris glow, with a
primary and secondary action and an optional product screenshot framed by a
gradient edge, so the value proposition lands before anything else.
**LogoCloud** immediately follows with low-commitment social proof —
recognizable customer wordmarks reassure the skeptic before they read on.

With trust seeded, **FeatureGrid** does the explaining: an icon-led 2- or
3-column grid of iris-tinted capability tiles that breaks the product into
scannable benefits. **Testimonial** then converts explanation into belief with
a single engineer's voice — one strong quote carries more weight than a wall of
capabilities. **Pricing** arrives once the visitor is convinced, presenting
usage-based tiers with one plan lifted by the iris ring to guide the choice.
**CTA** is the closing push: a gradient-edged card with its own grid and glow
that restates the offer with nothing to distract from the button. **Footer**
closes the page with navigation, legal, and the wordmark — the calm landing
after the pitch. The rhythm alternates dense and sparse — hero, thin logo
strip, dense grid, intimate quote, structured pricing, single-message CTA — so
the eye keeps moving toward the conversion.
