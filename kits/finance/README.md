# Finance

A data-forward design system for a trustworthy money product — an investing
app, digital bank, or wealth platform. The look is light, crisp, and precise:
deep ink type on clean near-white surfaces with a faint cool-green cast, a
single confident emerald-teal accent (`#0E8C6A`) used sparingly, and a
small-to-medium radius that reads as composed rather than playful. Inter
carries the interface while JetBrains Mono renders every figure with tabular
numerals, so numbers line up and read unmistakably as data. The result feels
like a regulated institution that happens to be beautifully designed — calm,
credible, and built to earn confidence.

Charts are a first-class element of this system, not an afterthought. The hero
frames a portfolio-growth line, a dedicated performance band stacks growth and
returns charts beside headline figures, and the chart blocks inherit the
emerald palette so data visualizations re-skin with the brand. Every editable
value flows through props, each section is a fluid full-width band, and motion
stays restrained — tasteful scroll-in reveals and gentle staggers — because
the goal is to move a visitor toward opening an account, not to dazzle them.

## Composition

The chain is a confidence-building narrative tuned for a financial product,
where trust must be earned before the ask. **Navbar** anchors the page and
keeps "Open account" in reach at all times. **Hero** states the core promise
beside a portfolio-growth chart, so the value proposition and the evidence for
it land together above the fold. **Metrics** immediately backs that promise
with hard numbers — assets, accounts, uptime, fees — rendered in mono so they
read as fact, not marketing.

With scale established, **Features** does the explaining: an icon-led grid that
breaks the platform into scannable capabilities. **Performance** is the
evidence section and the system's signature beat — charts of growth and annual
returns beside headline figures and an honest disclaimer, the proof a money
brand lives or dies by. **Security** follows in a deliberately sober register,
reassuring the skeptic on encryption, regulation, and protection before any
commitment is asked. Only then does **Pricing** present transparent, all-in
tiers with one recommended plan to steer the choice. **CTA** is the closing
push — a single emerald band that restates the offer with a reassurance note
and nothing to distract from the button. **Footer** lands the page with
navigation, social, copyright, and the regulatory disclaimer a financial
product is expected to carry. The rhythm alternates dense and sparse — hero,
metric row, feature grid, chart-led proof, trust grid, structured pricing,
single-message CTA — so the eye keeps moving toward the conversion.
