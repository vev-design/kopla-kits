# Portfolio

A refined, image-forward portfolio system for designers, studios, and makers:
warm paper ground, near-black ink, a single terracotta accent, generous
whitespace, and big confident type. Built to let the work carry the page —
project imagery leads, chrome stays quiet.

## Composition

Opens with a personal **Intro** (name, role, a one-line positioning
statement) so a visitor knows who they're looking at within seconds. The
**WorkGrid** is the spine — a scannable wall of covers — followed by one or
more **ProjectFeature** beats that slow down and go deep on a single piece
(alternating image side for rhythm). **About** is the human counterpoint to
the work, and **Contact** closes with a single clear way to reach out before
the quiet **Footer**. Reveal/stagger motion brings each section in on scroll
without stealing focus from the imagery.
